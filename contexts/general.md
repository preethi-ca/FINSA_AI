# About FINSA — Club Information

## Mission
FINSA (Finance Students' Association) is a student-run organization dedicated to finance education. We bridge the gap between academic finance and the real industry and help members build skills, networks, and experience.

## History
FINSA has been a cornerstone of finance education on campus for years, hosting events, workshops, speaker panels, and competitions. Our alumni work at top banks, funds, and corporates.

## Structure
We are run by an elected executive team (President, VP roles, portfolio leads) and supported by portfolio teams: **Finance**, **Design**, **Technology**, **External & Corporate Relations**, **Marketing**, **HR**, and **Events**. Membership is open to all students interested in finance.

## Culture
We value curiosity, rigour, and collaboration. We learn by doing: pitching ideas, getting feedback from industry mentors, and participating in competitions like Bull's Cage. We aim to be inclusive and supportive while maintaining high standards.
