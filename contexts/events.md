# Events

## Upcoming Events
Check the FINSA website and social media for the current semester's calendar: speaker panels, workshops, and socials. Dates and sign-up links are posted there.

## Past Events & Recaps
We run workshops (e.g. Excel, modelling, case prep), speaker panels with industry guests, and networking events. Past event recaps and highlights are often shared on our website or socials.

## Speaker Series
FINSA hosts a speaker series with professionals from banks, funds, and corporates. Topics range from career paths to market outlooks and deal discussions. Details for each term are on the website.

## Bull's Cage
Bull's Cage is our flagship stock pitch competition. Teams of 2–4 research and pitch a public equity idea to industry judges. It runs annually in the winter semester and is a great way to practice valuation, modelling, and presentation skills. Past winners have gone on to internships at top firms.
