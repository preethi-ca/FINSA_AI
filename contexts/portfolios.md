# Portfolio Descriptions (Informational Only)

These descriptions explain what each portfolio does. They are for information only — **portfolio placement is not chosen by applicants**. Placement happens during recruitment based on fit, performance, and committee decisions.

- **Public Equity**: Focuses on listed stocks, equity research, and fundamental analysis. Members build models, write research notes, and present pitches.
- **Private Equity**: Covers private companies, LBO modelling, and deal analysis. Good fit if you enjoy valuation and understanding capital structures.
- **Fixed Income**: Bonds, credit analysis, and interest rate strategy. Suited for those interested in rates, credit, and macro.
- **Quantitative Finance**: Data-driven and algorithmic strategies. Involves coding, backtesting, and systematic approaches.
- **Operations**: Supports club logistics, events, and communications. Keeps FINSA running smoothly.

Each portfolio meets weekly and presents to industry mentors. If you have questions about what a portfolio does or what kind of work fits each, we can help you understand — but we cannot guarantee or promise placement in any specific portfolio.
