# Portfolio Descriptions (Informational Only)

These descriptions explain what each portfolio does. They are for information only — **portfolio placement is not chosen by applicants**. Placement happens during recruitment based on fit, performance, and committee decisions.

- **Finance**: Handles treasury, budgeting, and financial planning for the club. Members work on funding allocation, sponsorship tracking, and reporting. Good fit if you want hands-on experience with club finances and financial controls.
- **Design**: Owns visual identity, branding, and creative assets. Members produce graphics, social content, and materials for events and recruitment. Suited for those interested in visual design, brand consistency, and creative execution.
- **Technology**: Builds and maintains digital tools, the website, and tech infrastructure. Involves web development, automation, and supporting club operations with technology. Good fit if you enjoy coding, systems, or product-style work.
- **External & Corporate Relations**: Manages relationships with sponsors, alumni, and industry partners. Members coordinate outreach, partnership proposals, and stakeholder communication. Suited for those interested in business development and professional networks.
- **Marketing**: Drives awareness and engagement through campaigns, social media, and communications. Members plan content, run channels, and support recruitment and event promotion. Good fit if you like content, analytics, and audience growth.
- **HR**: Supports member experience, onboarding, and club culture. Members help with recruitment logistics, feedback, and initiatives that keep the community engaged. Suited for those interested in people operations and organizational culture.
- **Events**: Plans and runs workshops, speaker series, and socials. Members handle logistics, venues, and execution so that FINSA’s events run smoothly. Good fit if you enjoy project coordination and bringing people together.

Each portfolio meets regularly and works with the rest of the exec. If you have questions about what a portfolio does or what kind of work fits each, we can help you understand — but we cannot guarantee or promise placement in any specific portfolio.
