# Portfolios

FINSA manages several student-run investment portfolios:
- **Public Equity**: Focuses on listed stocks and equity research.
- **Private Equity**: Analyzes private companies and LBO models.
- **Fixed Income**: Covers bonds, credit analysis, and interest rate strategy.
- **Quantitative Finance**: Uses data-driven and algorithmic strategies.

Each portfolio meets weekly and presents pitches to industry mentors.
