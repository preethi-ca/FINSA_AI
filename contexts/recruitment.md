# Recruitment Intelligence

## Hiring Cycle Guidance
- FINSA's main recruitment cycle runs every **September**.
- Applications open on the **Join Us** page on the FINSA website.
- Timeline: applications open → deadline → résumé review → interviews → offers. Exact dates are posted on the website and socials each year.
- We recruit for roles across Public Equity, Private Equity, Fixed Income, Quantitative Finance, and Operations.

## Application Tips
- **Résumé**: Keep it one page, clear formatting. Highlight relevant coursework, projects, and any finance or analytical experience. Quantify where you can.
- **Cover letter**: Short and genuine. Explain why you're interested in FINSA and what you hope to learn or contribute. We care about fit and motivation.
- **Interview prep**: Be ready to discuss your interest in finance, your background, and why FINSA. Some roles may include a short case or discussion; we'll communicate format before the interview.
- **Common questions**: "Tell us about yourself," "Why FINSA?," "What draws you to [portfolio/role]?," "What's a recent market or deal you found interesting?"

## Portfolio Fit Insights
- Different roles suit different interests: research vs. modelling vs. quant vs. operations. We can help you understand what each portfolio does so you can express informed preferences.
- **We do not promise placement in a specific portfolio.** Placement is decided during recruitment based on fit, performance, and committee evaluation. Being informed helps you prepare and interview well; it does not guarantee a particular role.

## FAQs
- **Can I choose my portfolio?** No. You can express interest and we consider fit, but placement is determined during recruitment based on your performance and our assessment of where you'll thrive.
- **When do applications open?** Typically September; check the Join Us page and our socials for exact dates.
- **Who can apply?** Membership is open to all students interested in finance; specific eligibility (e.g. year, program) is stated on the application each cycle.
- **Where do I apply?** On the FINSA website, Join Us page. Submit résumé and cover letter as instructed.
- **Questions about hiring?** Email recruitment@finsa.ca.
